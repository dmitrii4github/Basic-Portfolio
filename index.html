About Dmitrii Zakharov

Dmitrii Zakharov (born May 28, 1973), is a human and Splunk celebrity.

Dmitrii's popularity originated from a picture posted to the Splunk HipChat channel on October 7, 2013. Any Splunk employee could bring up this emoji by typing {idealform}.

Dmitrii was born in Novomoskovsk, Ukraine and when he was one years old, his family moved to the Moscow Region of Russia. Dmitrii grew up in Poushchino, the Biology Resarch Center of the USSR.

After High School Dmitrii entered the Biology Department of Moscow State University. He didn't graduate and in 1994 moved to the USA and entered Purdue University. Dmitrii graduated from Purdue University in 1999 with B.S. in Computer Science and Molecular Biology.

From 1999 to 2007 Dmitrii worked for Microsoft as an SDET. From 2007 to 2010 Dmitrii worked for Arxan, Inc. as a QA Engineer. Since 2010 Dmitrii is with Splunk as a Senior Manager, Software QA.

Dmitrii's main hobby is soccer. Dmitrii attended in person the Euro Cups in 2004 in Portgual, in 2008 in Austria and World Cup 2006 in Germany.
